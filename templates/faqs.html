About Swoll with Samsung: Frequently asked questions

Q: How do I request a new exercise?

A: Simply go the the request tab to propose a new exercise, which will then be send to the council for deliberation. If they choose to accept your request, it will be assigned a point value and be added for everone to use.

Q: How do I edit my exercises

A: Click on the exercises tab, select the exercise you want to add to, and enter the quantity. If you are done, press done, otherwise press add another exercise.
